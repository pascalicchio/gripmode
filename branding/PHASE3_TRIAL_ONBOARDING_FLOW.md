# Gripmode Trial Onboarding Flow (Low-Touch)

## Objective
Get new trials to first value in <15 minutes and first operational win in <24 hours.

## Onboarding Steps
1. **Create account**
2. **Set school profile** (name, type, location count)
3. **Import core data** (students/classes basic setup)
4. **Enable first retention rule**
5. **Run first operations dashboard check**

## Activation Milestones
- A1: School profile completed
- A2: First students/classes loaded
- A3: First retention/risk action taken
- A4: Team member invited

## In-App Guidance
- Progress bar with 4 milestones
- “Next best action” card
- Short setup checklists (no long tutorials)

## Friction Reduction Rules
- Keep forms short
- Use defaults where possible
- Defer advanced settings
- Offer quick templates by school type

## Conversion Triggers
- Day 3: “You’re 1 step from full setup”
- Day 7: “See retention impact from your first rule”
- Day 12: “Trial ending: keep your workflows live”
