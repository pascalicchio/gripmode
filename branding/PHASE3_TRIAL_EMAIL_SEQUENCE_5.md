# Gripmode 5-Email Trial Activation Sequence

## Email 1 — Welcome (Day 0)
Subject: Welcome to Gripmode — let’s get your school set up
- Confirm trial started
- 3-step quick-start
- CTA: Complete setup

## Email 2 — First Win (Day 1)
Subject: Get your first operational win today
- Focus on one high-value action (retention rule)
- Explain outcome
- CTA: Enable first retention workflow

## Email 3 — Social Proof + Clarity (Day 3)
Subject: Why school owners switch to Gripmode
- Built by multi-location owners
- Efficient, affordable, practical
- CTA: Finish activation milestones

## Email 4 — Value Expansion (Day 7)
Subject: Run your school with less noise
- Show 2–3 advanced but relevant capabilities
- Position around leadership time saved
- CTA: Invite your team

## Email 5 — Conversion (Day 12)
Subject: Keep your system live after trial
- Trial ending reminder
- Recap value achieved
- Reinforce low-complexity / high-control promise
- CTA: Choose your plan

## Tone Rules
- Direct and disciplined
- No hype language
- Outcome-first
- Always one clear CTA per email
