Gripmode Pricing Structure (Self-Serve)

Pricing Strategy Goals

  • Low-touch sales motion
  • Fast trial-to-paid conversion
  • Clear value by school size

Plan Structure (recommended)

Starter

For early-stage schools

  • Core operations
  • Basic retention alerts
  • 1 location
  • Email support

Growth (Recommended)

For established schools scaling operations

  • Everything in Starter
  • Advanced retention workflows
  • Better reporting
  • Team permissions
  • 1 location + optional add-ons

Multi-Location

For owners with 2+ schools

  • Everything in Growth
  • Multi-location management
  • Cross-location reporting
  • Centralized controls
  • Priority support

Pricing Principles

  • Publish transparent monthly pricing
  • 14-day free trial
  • No sales call required
  • Annual option with discount

Core Offer Mechanics

  • Primary CTA: Start Free Trial
  • Secondary CTA: Compare Plans
  • Risk reducer: Cancel anytime during trial

Upgrade Triggers

  • Student count thresholds
  • Need for advanced reporting
  • Multi-staff complexity
  • Multi-location expansion
